Dan Marino

I'm a software engineer in New York City, with experience in Ruby, JavaScript/Node.js, PostgreSQL, AWS, Rails, and React.

Most recently, I built Haven, a Secrets Manager for Node applications.

Haven is an open-source, centralized secrets manager.

It protects your application secrets through a combination of encryption, access control, and injection-at-runtime. It’s easy to set up, and offers an intuitive GUI to set fine-grained access controls and to view logs. Haven allows small teams to securely manage all of their projects’ secrets---and to do so with a minimum of hassle so that they can get back to developing their applications without sacrificing security.
